Firefox

Right click on the URL bar and choose Add "Search". This will add the Redirection Server as one of the viable search engines. To make this one the default search engine follow these steps:
  • Click the hamburger button in the top right of the screen.
  • Select Settings.
  • In the Settings tab that opens, click on Search in the left pane.
  • In the center pane look for Default Search Engine and choose RS.
